A simple HTML drawing app completely vibe coded for my kids to use without adverts.

## What is it?

A clean, distraction-free finger painting app that works great on phones and tablets. No tracking, no ads, no nonsense - just a canvas and some colors.

## Features

- **Multi-touch drawing** - Multiple fingers can draw at the same time with different colors
- **Big, colorful buttons** - Easy for little hands to tap
- **Simple controls**:
  - 5 bright colors (red, blue, green, yellow, black)
  - 3 brush sizes (small, medium, large)
  - Undo button to fix mistakes
  - Clear button to start over
- **Mobile-friendly** - Works perfectly on phones and tablets with touch screens
- **Collapsible toolbar** - Hide the buttons to get more drawing space

## How to use

Just open `index.html` in a web browser and start drawing! Works best on a touchscreen device.

## Why this exists

Made this for my kids so they could draw on my phone/tablet without being bombarded with ads or accidentally clicking on in-app purchases. Sometimes you just need a simple paint app that does one thing well.
